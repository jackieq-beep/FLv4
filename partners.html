What You Need, Kept Simple

High-level signals on why in-game works, what inventory exists, and how reporting is handled.

Why In-Game Works 3 signals
  • Lean-in attention: visibility during active play — not passive scrolling.
  • Repetition: exposure compounds across matches, sessions, and replays.
  • Context: placements live inside broadcast-style moments (kickoff, goals, highlights).
Sponsorship Inventory high level
  • Match Presented By (intro, scoreboard, replays, match flow moments)
  • Always-On placements (stadium + UI, brand-safe, persistent)
  • Broadcast moments (walkouts, lineups, halftime, player of the match)

If you want, link your full inventory schedule as a PDF or a private deck after first contact.

Measurement & Reporting credible
  • Viewable exposure: branding is rendered in the visible viewport.
  • Time-based visibility: supports duration thresholds where relevant.
  • Reporting cadence: match-level + campaign summaries (weekly/monthly).
